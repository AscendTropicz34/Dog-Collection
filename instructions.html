 Quick start Guide 

1

 Create a MetaMask wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile phone. That will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive ETH. 

2

  Purchase ETH to add to your wallet's balance

You can buy ETH directly on MetaMask or transfer it to your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, etc.

3

 Connect your wallet to OpenSea

Access OpenSea's website by clicking the more drpdown and then OpenSea where you can click on the person icon in the top right corner and then click profile, this will then redirect you to choose out of the four different wallets availabe, select metmask and allow it to link with opensea by clicking sign to approve the linking.    

4

  Purchase the NFT   

To purchase an NFT that we have created part of this collection select one of the NFTs you would like in the doggo people collection on OpenSea.
